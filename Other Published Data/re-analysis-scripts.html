Re-Analysis of Other Published Data

Dave Bridges

September 6, 2015

This script is for the re-analysis of previously published data from other groups. This is often for presentations wherein the data was presented as a table or something and we want to draw attention to a particular aspect. This script was most recently run on Mon Sep 7 10:25:45 2015.

Ebbeling et al JAMA 2012 Paper

The paper (Ebbeling et al. 2012) used subjects who lost 12% of their weight, then had their weight stabilized. They used a crossover design to test weight maintenance on low carbohydrate, or high carbohydrate diet. They also used a high glycemic index diet that we are ignoring here.

The data from Table 2 of Ebbeling et al. (2012) was re-entered as Ebbeling JAMA 2012 Table 3.csv.

Bray et al Overfeeding Study

In George A. Bray et al. (2012), subjects were overfed for 8 weeks with equal calories of low, normal (not included here) and high protein diet. Total energy expenditure is taken from the Table in this paper.

The data from the table was re-entered as Bray JAMA 2012 Table.csv.

Bray et al Follow-Up Study

In a follow up study, G. A. Bray et al. (2015) looked at the energy expenditure in a tissue specific manner via DEXA scans.
